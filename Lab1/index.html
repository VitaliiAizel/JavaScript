Explanation

You can solve for the sides and acute angles of a right
triangle. To do this, enter the word 'triangle', after which in round
brackets write the value (for a correct result, enter only the correct
values) and the type of two data, including the following:

'leg' and 'leg'

'leg' and 'hypotenuse'

'leg' and 'adjacent angle'

'leg' and 'opposite angle'

'hypotenuse' and 'angle'

(the order of entering the arguments is not important)

'For example you can enter this: triangle(5,"hypotenuse",3,"leg");
(But you can do it only in console (tap F12 for it))
